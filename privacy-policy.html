1. Introduction

Welcome to Anaya QR Scanner ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By downloading, installing, or using Anaya QR Scanner, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be absolutely clear about what information we do NOT collect:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
  • No Camera Data: While the App uses your device's camera to scan QR codes and barcodes, the camera feed is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.
  • No Internet Permissions: The App does not require internet access to function. All scanning and processing occurs locally on your device.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: We do not use any analytics, tracking tools, or advertising SDKs that would collect usage data.

3. Information Processed Locally

The App processes the following information locally on your device only:

3.1 Scan Results

  • QR code and barcode content that you scan
  • Scan history (if you choose to save scans)
  • Scan timestamps and scan type (QR Code or Barcode)

3.2 Device Storage

  • Scan history is stored locally on your device using the device's internal storage
  • No scan data is backed up to cloud services or transmitted externally
  • Data is stored using Android's SharedPreferences system

4. Permissions & Their Purpose

Permission Purpose Control
Camera To scan QR codes and barcodes. The camera is used only for real-time scanning. No images or video are saved or transmitted. You can deny this permission, but the App's scanning functionality will not work.
Storage (Optional) To save scan history locally on your device and potentially export scans. This is an optional permission that you can deny. You can still use scanning features without it.

6. Children's Privacy

Anaya QR Scanner is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information stored locally on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Access and Control

  • View your scan history within the App
  • Clear individual scan history items (if implemented)
  • Clear all App data through device settings

8.2 Permission Management

  • Grant or revoke camera permissions at any time through device settings
  • Grant or revoke storage permissions at any time through device settings

8.3 Data Deletion

  • Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data
  • Clear App data through device settings (Settings > Apps > Anaya QR Scanner > Storage > Clear Data)
